# Announcement on USDT-APTOS Network Withdrawal Suspension by Bitget

Bitget has announced the suspension of USDT-APTOS network withdrawal services starting on September 25, 2025, at 21:41 (UTC+8). This move is to enhance security and stability. Trading remains unaffected during this period.

Why the Suspension?
The pause aims to address potential vulnerabilities and implement necessary improvements such as enhanced transactional efficiency and security protocols. For updates, stay informed through Bitget's official channels:
